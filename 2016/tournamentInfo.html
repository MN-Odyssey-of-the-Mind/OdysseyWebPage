MN State Tournament Information - 2016

Once your group has registered as a member of Odyssey of the Mind, you will probably want to consider participating in the MN State Tournament. Because our tournament is completely run by volunteers, we require each team to provide a volunteer to help our tournament run smoothly. As you look ahead to the state tournament, please keep this in mind. You can find out more about the volunteer requirement here.

The 2016 MN Odyssey of the Mind State Tournament was held on:

March 12, 2016 at
Friendly Hills Middle School,
701 Mendota Heights Rd,
Mendota Heights, MN 55120.
