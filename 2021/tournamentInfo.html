MN State Tournament Information - 2021

Once your group has registered as a member of Odyssey of the Mind, it's time to start preparing for the Minnesota State Tournament!

Our tournament, and our whole organization, is completely run by volunteers. We require each team to provide a volunteer or judge to help our tournament run smoothly -- please keep this in mind as you look ahead to the state tournament. Find more information about this requirement here.

To register for the Minnesota State Tournament (by January 31), please follow the instructions in step 2 on our "Get Started" page.
